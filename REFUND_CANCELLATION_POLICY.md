**Last Updated: March 2026**

## 1. Overview

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which AcadeMatt provides refunds, handles session cancellations, and manages unused service packages. All policies are processed in accordance with consumer protection standards and the terms outlined in our Terms of Service.

---

## 2. Individual Session Refund Policy

### 2.1 Cancellation with Full Refund

**You are eligible for a full refund if you:**
- Cancel your session **at least 24 hours before** the scheduled session time
- Submit cancellation through your AcadeMatt account dashboard
- Have not previously violated cancellation terms

**Refund Timeline:**
- Full refunds are processed within **7-10 business days** to your original payment method
- If refund does not appear, contact support within 30 days

### 2.2 Cancellation with Partial Refund

**You are eligible for a 50% partial refund if you:**
- Cancel your session **between 12-24 hours** before the scheduled time
- Submit cancellation through the Platform

**Partial Refund Logic:**
- Original session cost (e.g., $50) = 50% refund ($25)
- Processing time: 7-10 business days

### 2.3 Cancellation with No Refund (No-Show Policy)

**No refund is issued:**
- **If you do not cancel** and fail to join the session (no-show)
- If you join **more than 15 minutes late** without prior notification
- If a tutor cancels due to your disruptive behavior
- If you cancel **within 12 hours** of the session start time

**Definition of No-Show:**
- Student does not join the Google Meet session
- Session time passes with no communication from student
- Student was not marked absent by the tutor prior to session start

**Consequence:** Session cost is forfeited and cannot be refunded or rescheduled.

### 2.4 Tutor-Initiated Cancellation

**If AcadeMatt or the assigned tutor cancels:**
- Full refund issued automatically
- Session is automatically rescheduled (with your consent)
- If rescheduling is not possible, refund is processed within 5 business days

**Reasons for Tutor Cancellation:**
- Tutor illness or emergency
- Technical issues on tutor side
- Medical or safety concerns

## 3. Package Refund Policy

### 3.1 Unused Package Refunds

AcadeMatt offers several package options:
- **5-Hour Pack**
- **10-Hour Pack**
- **Larger custom packages**

**Refund Eligibility for Unused Hours:**
- Packages may be refunded if **unused and within 14 days** of purchase
- Refund = (# of unused hours) × (hourly rate)
- Partial usage = only unused hours are refundable

**Example:**
```
Purchase: 5-Hour Pack = $250 (at $50/hour)
Sessions completed: 1 hour
Sessions cancelled/rescheduled: 1 hour
Hours remaining: 3 hours
Refund eligible: $150 (3 hours × $50)
```

### 3.2 Package Expiration

- **Packages expire 12 months** after purchase
- Expired, unused hours are **forfeited with no refund**
- We do not extend expiration retroactively
- Unused time does not roll over to new purchases

## 4. Payment Refund Process

### 4.1 How to Request a Refund

1. **Log into your AcadeMatt account**
2. **Navigate to "Sessions" or "Account"**
3. **Select the session or package** you wish to refund
4. **Click "Request Refund"** and confirm

Alternatively: Email **matias.academatt@gmail.com** with:
- Your full name
- Session date/time or package reference
- Reason for refund
- Original order/payment confirmation

### 4.2 Refund Processing

- **Review period**: 2-3 business days
- **Processing time**: 7-10 business days after approval
- **Refund method**: Original payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
- **Currency**: Refunded in the same currency as original purchase

**Note:** Refunds are processed by **Paddle** (our payment processor). Contact Paddle if refund doesn't appear in your account after 10 business days.

### 4.3 Refund Status Tracking

- Check refund status in your AcadeMatt account under "Transactions"
- Email support for refund status inquiries
- Response within 2 business days

## 5. Rescheduling vs. Cancellation

### 5.1 Rescheduling (Preferred)

**Instead of cancelling, you can reschedule your session:**
- **No fee** for rescheduling if done 24+ hours before session
- **$10 fee** for rescheduling within 24 hours of session
- **Rescheduled sessions do NOT expire** with the original package timeline

**How to Reschedule:**
1. Log into your account
2. Select the session
3. Choose "Reschedule"
4. Pick your new date and time
5. Confirm

### 5.2 Important Notes

- Rescheduling is free and does not consume additional package hours
- You can reschedule **up to 3 times per package**
- After 3 rescheduling attempts, the hour is forfeited unless you cancel for a refund

### No Refunds Issued For:

- **Academic Performance**: Poor grades or exam results

- **Change of Mind**: Deciding you no longer need tutoring

## 11. Refund FAQ

**Q: Can I get a refund after attending a session?**
A: No. Once a session is completed, it is non-refundable. Refunds are for cancelled sessions only.

**Q: What if the tutor joined late?**
A: If the tutor joined more than 15 minutes late without notice:
- You may request a partial refund for late arrival
- Or choose to reschedule the portion missed
- Contact support with session details

**Q: Do package hours expire?**
A: Yes, all hours expire 12 months from purchase. Unused hours are forfeited.

**Q: Can I transfer my package to another person?**
A: No. Packages are non-transferable. Only the account holder may use purchased hours.

**Q: How long does a refund take?**
A: 7-10 business days after approval.

**Q: What if my payment method is no longer valid?**
A: Refunds may be delayed. Contact Paddle or your bank for assistance.

**Q: Can I get a refund if I'm unhappy with the tutor's teaching style?**
A: This is not grounds for automatic refund. Instead:
- Request a different tutor for future sessions
- Attend at least one session before assessing fit
- Contact support if serious issues arise
