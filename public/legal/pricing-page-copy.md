## FAQ Section (Optional but Recommended)

### **Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q: Can I change my tutor if I don't feel a good fit?**
A: Yes! In your account settings, you can request a different tutor for future sessions. There's no fee to switch.

**Q: What if I need to cancel a session?**
A: You have a 24-hour cancellation window for a full refund. Sessions cancelled within 12-24 hours receive a 50% refund. No refund for no-shows after 15 minutes. See our full Refund & Cancellation Policy for details.

**Q: Do my unused hours expire?**
A: Yes. Hours are valid for 12 months from purchase. After 12 months, unused hours expire. Plan accordingly!

**Q: Can a session be longer than 1 hour?**
A: Absolutely. You can book 1-hour sessions or request custom duration (2 hours, 90 minutes, etc.). Pricing adjusts accordingly.

**Q: What technology do I need?**
A: Just a device (laptop, tablet, phone) with a camera, microphone, and stable internet. Google Meet is web-based – no download needed.

**Q: Can I gift a package to someone else?**
A: Not directly. However, you can purchase a package and share the access details. Packages are tied to accounts, so the gift recipient would need their own AcadeMatt account.

**Q: Are there discounts for schools or large groups?**
A: Yes! Contact our sales team for bulk and institutional rates. We offer special pricing for 20+ hour bundles.

**Q: Is there a student discount or scholarship?**
A: We occasionally offer promotions. Sign up for our newsletter for updates on discounts and special offers.

**Q: What subjects do you tutor?**
A: We specialize in mathematics (algebra through calculus) and sciences (physics, chemistry, biology). For other subjects, contact support for availability.

**Q: What about lesson preparation or homework help?**
A: Our tutors help you *understand* concepts and approach problems. We don't do your homework *for* you – that's against our academic integrity policy. We focus on building your problem-solving skills.
